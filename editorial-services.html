Editorial Services

Strong writing deserves strong editing. Whether you are polishing a novel, preparing a business report, or finalizing web content, the right editorial service ensures your work is clear, consistent, and compelling. Here’s what each level of editing offers:

Developmental Editing

Developmental editing focuses on the big picture. It is about shaping your manuscript at the structural and conceptual level. Instead of correcting commas, this service asks:

  • Does the structure flow logically?
  • Are the ideas fully developed and engaging?
  • Is the tone right for your audience?
  • Are there gaps, redundancies, or sections that need to be reworked?

You will receive feedback on organization, clarity, and overall impact, often with suggestions for revisions. Developmental editing is ideal if you want to strengthen your content before worrying about sentence-level polish.

Copyediting

Copyediting works at the sentence level. Once your content is structurally sound, this service ensures consistency, clarity, and correctness. A copyeditor will:

  • Fix grammar, spelling, and punctuation errors.
  • Smooth awkward phrasing and tighten sentences.
  • Standardize style and formatting (based on your style guide, if applicable).
  • Eliminate wordiness and maintain a consistent tone.

Copyediting makes your writing clean and professional while preserving your voice.

Proofreading

Proofreading is the final check before publication. It is not about rewriting—it is about catching the small mistakes that slip through. A proofreader will:

  • Correct typos, spelling errors, and minor grammar issues.
  • Check for formatting inconsistencies.
  • Ensure punctuation and capitalization are accurate and consistent.

Proofreading is the last line of defense to ensure your work is polished, error-free, and ready to be published or submitted.

Copyediting

Proofreading

Level of Detail: Low

Pricing

Our pricing is flexible and tailored to your project. Costs vary depending on three key factors: word count (longer manuscripts require more time), complexity (technical or highly academic texts need extra care), and turnaround (rush projects are billed at a premium). This way, you only pay for the work you truly need.

To help you save time and money, we also offer bundled packages. For example, combining Developmental Editing + Copyediting streamlines revisions, while a Copyediting + Proofreading bundle ensures no detail is overlooked. Bundles are not only cost-effective but also create a smoother, faster editing process with one editorial partner guiding your work.
