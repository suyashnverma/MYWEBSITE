Design Production

Design is the bridge between your manuscript and your reader. From the way text flows on the page to the visual appeal of your cover, great design ensures your book is professional, engaging, and ready for the marketplace. Here’s how our design services can support your vision:

Book Layout & Typesetting

The inside of your book deserves as much attention as the cover. With professional layout and typesetting, we ensure your content is visually clear and reader-friendly:

  • Clean formatting for print and digital editions.
  • Proper font selection, spacing, and margins.
  • Consistent headings, captions, and footnotes.
  • Attention to academic/research requirements where applicable.

This service makes your book polished, readable, and aligned with industry standards.

Cover Design

Your cover is your first impression — it needs to grab attention and convey the essence of your book. Our designers provide:

  • Custom illustrations or photo-based designs.
  • Typography that enhances the theme of your book.
  • Front, back, and spine design aligned with publishing requirements.
  • Print-ready and digital-friendly versions.

A professional cover elevates your book, making it stand out on shelves and online stores.

Illustrations & Graphics

Whether you are working on children's literature, educational content, or technical documentation, illustrations and graphics bring your ideas to life:

  • Custom artwork and visual storytelling.
  • Graphs, charts, and infographics for clarity.
  • Integration with text for a seamless reading experience.
  • Style tailored to your audience and genre.

This service ensures your content is not only informative but also visually compelling.

Pricing

Our design pricing depends on the scope (number of pages, illustrations, or complexity of the design), format (print vs. digital), and customization level (standard layouts vs. bespoke design). This ensures you pay only for what you need.

To help streamline your project, we also offer bundled packages. For example, combining Cover Design + Layout ensures consistent branding inside and out, while Layout + Illustrations is perfect for research and children’s books. Bundles are both cost-effective and time-efficient, handled by one design team.
