Marketing & Promotion

Publishing a book is only half the journey — the other half is making sure it reaches readers. Our Marketing & Promotion services are designed to amplify your book’s presence, build your author brand, and connect your story with the right audience. From launch campaigns to long-term visibility strategies, we tailor every plan to your goals and readership.

Author Branding

Your name is your brand. We help you establish a professional identity that builds trust and recognition across platforms:

  • Develop a consistent author persona and message.
  • Audit and enhance your social media presence.
  • Design branded assets like logos, banners, and templates.
  • Create strategies for long-term visibility and growth.

Strong branding ensures your audience remembers you — not just your book.

Book Launch Campaigns

Launches set the tone for your book’s success. Our campaigns are designed to create buzz and momentum:

  • Pre-release teasers and cover reveals.
  • Coordinated social media promotions.
  • Influencer and blogger outreach.
  • Virtual and in-person launch events.

A strong launch maximizes visibility and sets your book up for long-term sales.

Digital Marketing

Expand your reach and measure your impact with data-driven digital strategies:

  • Amazon and Goodreads advertising.
  • Google, Facebook, and Instagram ad campaigns.
  • Video teasers and book trailers.
  • Landing pages and lead-generation funnels.

Digital marketing ensures your book connects with readers where they already are — online.

PR & Media Outreach

Credibility matters. We help you tap into traditional and digital media networks:

  • Press releases for book launches and milestones.
  • Pitching to newspapers, magazines, and radio shows.
  • Securing podcast and interview opportunities.
  • Coordinating professional book reviews.

Media coverage builds authority and positions you as an expert in your genre or field.

Events & Community Engagement

Books thrive when they spark conversations. We create opportunities for you to connect directly with readers:

  • Book signings, readings, and Q&A sessions.
  • Participation in book fairs and literary festivals.
  • Community book club partnerships.
  • Virtual events for global reach.

Engagement builds loyal readership and fosters long-term connections.

Content Marketing & SEO

Keep your audience engaged long after launch through consistent content strategies:

  • Blog posts, newsletters, and podcasts.
  • Search engine optimization for discoverability.
  • Content calendars tailored to your audience.
  • Evergreen content that builds lasting visibility.

Content marketing ensures your voice stays active in conversations that matter.

Marketing Campaign Timeline

A successful promotion unfolds in stages. Here’s a sample roadmap we use to plan and execute effective campaigns:

Pre-Launch

  • Build author platform (social media, website, newsletter).
  • Create anticipation with cover reveals and teaser content.
  • Secure early reviews and endorsements (ARCs, beta readers).
  • Plan blog tours, podcast interviews, or press outreach.

Launch

  • Coordinate book release date across platforms.
  • Host virtual or physical launch events.
  • Leverage paid ads (Amazon, social media, Google).
  • Engage readers with giveaways, contests, or special discounts.

Post-Launch

  • Maintain momentum with continued ads and promotions.
  • Encourage reviews on Amazon, Goodreads, and other platforms.
  • Pitch for long-term opportunities (book clubs, libraries, schools).
  • Develop evergreen marketing (SEO blog posts, content marketing).
