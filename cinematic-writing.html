Book vs. Screenplay

Book

  • Explores inner thoughts and backstory.
  • Prose-driven, descriptive narration.
  • Flexible length and pacing.
  • Audience: readers who imagine the world.

Screenplay

  • Shows action and dialogue visually.
  • Strict formatting (industry standard).
  • Tight pacing (usually 90–120 pages).
  • Audience: directors, actors, producers.
