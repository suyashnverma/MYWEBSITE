Legal & Administrative Services

Publishing is not just about creativity — it’s also about protecting your rights, managing contracts, and ensuring compliance. Our Legal & Administrative services provide authors with the foundation needed to publish confidently, safeguard their intellectual property, and navigate the publishing world without unnecessary risks.

Copyright & ISBN

Your work deserves protection and recognition. We assist with:

  • Registering copyright for legal protection.
  • Acquiring ISBNs for print and digital editions.
  • Guidance on ISBN usage across formats and regions.

Proper registration safeguards your book from misuse and ensures it can be distributed professionally worldwide.

Contracts & Agreements

Clear agreements prevent conflicts later. Our support includes:

  • Author–publisher contracts (royalties, rights, responsibilities).
  • Collaboration agreements for co-authors, illustrators, or translators.
  • Custom contract reviews and advice.

We help ensure your contracts are fair, transparent, and aligned with your publishing goals.

Rights Management

Control how, where, and in what formats your book is published:

  • Territorial rights (domestic vs. international distribution).
  • Format rights: print, eBook, and audiobook.
  • Guidance on negotiating rights sales.

Effective rights management maximizes your book’s reach and revenue potential.

Permissions & Licensing

Publishing often involves external content. We help with:

  • Securing permissions for third-party text, images, or media.
  • Advising on public domain vs. copyrighted material.
  • Licensing agreements for safe content usage.

This ensures your work is legally compliant and safe to publish worldwide.

Administrative Essentials

Stay compliant with publishing standards and obligations:

  • Deposits with national libraries (legal deposit obligations).
  • Metadata & cataloging-in-publication (CIP) setup.
  • Recordkeeping, taxation, and royalty reporting guidance.

These tasks may seem small, but they ensure your book meets professional and legal standards globally.

Author Protection

Publishing can sometimes lead to disputes — we help you prepare:

  • Plagiarism detection and prevention.
  • Advice on handling disputes and arbitration.
  • Guidance on safeguarding moral and financial rights.

Our goal is to protect you, your book, and your long-term career as an author.

Which Legal Support Do You Need?

Level of Detail: Straightforward but essential

Contracts & Rights

Best For: Authors signing publishing or collaboration deals

Focus: Fair terms, royalties, rights ownership

Level of Detail: High, requires careful review

Final Step?: Before publication agreements

Permissions & Licensing

Best For: Authors using third-party material

Focus: Legal clearance, licensing safety

Level of Detail: Case-specific

Pricing

Legal and administrative needs vary widely, which is why our pricing depends on complexity (a simple copyright registration vs. multi-party contracts), scope (number of rights/licenses to handle), and jurisdiction (domestic vs. international legal processes).

We also provide bundled packages for efficiency. For example, Copyright + ISBN setup ensures your book is both protected and ready for distribution, while a Contracts + Rights Management bundle gives you peace of mind when entering publishing agreements.
