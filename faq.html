Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

For Clients

We offer a wide range of publishing and writing services, including academic writing support, SoPs, research assistance, translations, editing, screenwriting, dialogue writing, poster design, subtitles, and more. Each project is tailored to your unique needs.
Contributors are independent professionals carefully vetted by Satyartha Publishing. They bring expertise in writing, editing, translation, design, and creative fields. By working with contributors directly under our open-sourcing model, we are able to keep publishing costs affordable without compromising quality. There are no hidden charges — everything is transparent.
When you hire a contributor, your payment is held safely by Satyartha Publishing, similar to an escrow system. The funds are released to the contributor only when you confirm that you’re satisfied with the delivered work. A small administrative fee is retained by us to cover facilitation costs.
If you are not happy with the work, you can request revisions from the contributor. If issues remain unresolved, Satyartha Publishing steps in as an arbitrator to ensure fairness. Refunds or adjustments will be handled according to the client–contributor contract.

For Contributors

If you are a writer, editor, translator, or creative professional, you can apply through our contributor onboarding process. Once vetted and approved, you’ll have the opportunity to work on projects under the Satyartha Publishing banner.
Once you complete the project and the client approves it, we release your payment from the safely held funds. A small service fee is retained by the company, while the rest is paid directly to you.
We make sure contributors are never left uncompensated. If a client withdraws or fails to uphold their contractual terms, you will still be paid fairly from the safely held funds for the work already completed and expenses incurred.
Our escrow-style system balances both sides. Contributors know their efforts will be compensated, while clients know their funds are secure until they’re satisfied with the work. In case of disagreements, we step in as neutral arbitrators to ensure fairness.
Contributors work independently but are expected to follow our publishing and ethical guidelines to maintain quality and professionalism. Misconduct may lead to removal from the contributor network.
